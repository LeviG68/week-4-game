You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four Bufflo below.
By clicking on the "Buffalo's" you will add a specfic amont of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to random numbers, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each Buffalo is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each Buffalo.
